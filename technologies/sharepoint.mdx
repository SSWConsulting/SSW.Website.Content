SharePoint is Microsoft's on premise platform for document management, records management, [content management](https://www.ssw.com.au/ssw/Consulting/ContentManagement.aspx) and workflow. It improves organizational effectiveness by providing one unified suite of enterprise-scale applications. This satisfies diverse business-critical needs by simplifying how people work together across boundaries and enabling better informed decision-making.
